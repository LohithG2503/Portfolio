# **App Name**: Talent Showcase

## Core Features:

- Skills & Expertise Display: Dedicated section to showcase skills and expertise with progress bars and proficiency levels.
- Project Gallery: Interactive project gallery with detailed views, including project descriptions, technologies used, and live demo links.
- Education & Certifications Timeline: Chronological display of education and certifications with dates and descriptions.
- 'About Me' Page: Comprehensive 'About Me' section with a personal bio, contact information, and social media links.
- AI Resume Refinement: An AI-powered tool to refine the content of the user's resume to maximize its effectiveness. This might take into account data such as job performance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Light Lavender (#a78bfa) to provide a calm and sophisticated background.
- Background color: White (#FFFFFF) with a slight opacity applied via a frosted glass/glassmorphism effect.
- Accent color: Dark Lavender (#D8BFD8) for interactive elements and highlights to maintain a cohesive aesthetic.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font known for its clean and readable design.
- Simple, minimalist icons in a lavender hue to complement the overall design.
- Modern, minimalist layout with clear, defined sections and ample white space to enhance readability.
- Subtle animations and transitions on hover and page load to add a touch of interactivity without overwhelming the user.
